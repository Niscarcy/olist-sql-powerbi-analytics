# Analytics Data Model

To support BI reporting and dashboards, an analytics schema was created.

The analytics layer contains curated views designed for business analysis.

## Views

### kpi_sales_summary
Contains the main business KPIs:
- total_orders
- total_revenue
- avg_order_value

### sales_by_month
Monthly sales performance:
- total_orders
- revenue

### sales_by_state
Sales distribution by customer state.

### sales_by_category
Revenue generated by each product category.

### seller_performance
Performance of marketplace sellers:
- items sold
- revenue
